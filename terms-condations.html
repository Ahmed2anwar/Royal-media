Royal Media Club — Terms & Conditions

  1. 1-Acceptance of Terms

    By enrolling in Royal Media Club, the member acknowledges and agrees to be legally bound by these Terms & Conditions. Participation in the program constitutes full and unconditional acceptance of all rules outlined herein.

  2. 2-Eligibility

    Royal Media Club is available to approved Royal Media customers only. Royal Media reserves the right to approve, reject, suspend, or terminate membership at its sole discretion without prior notice.

  3. 3-Points Accumulation

    Members earn 1 point for every 1 EGP spent on eligible Royal Media products.

    Points are calculated based on the net invoice value excluding VAT, discounts, shipping, and service fees, unless otherwise stated.

    Points are credited only after full invoice payment is received.

    Royal Media reserves the right to review and adjust points in case of accounting discrepancies.

  4. 4-Bonus Reward Policy (Strict Policy)

    Upon accumulating every 250,000 valid points, the member becomes eligible for a bonus reward valued at 4,000 EGP.

    The reward may be redeemed as:

    • Free Royal Media products, OR
    • Additional discount applied to a future purchase.

    Important Conditions:

    • Bonus rewards cannot be partially redeemed.
    • Points must reach the full threshold of every 250,000 to redeem.
    • Bonus value is non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash.
    • Bonus must be redeemed within the validity period of the points.
    • Royal Media reserves the right to determine eligible products for redemption.
    • Management approval may be required before bonus application.

  5. 5-Points Validity

    All earned points are valid for 6 months from the date of issuance.

    Expired points are automatically forfeited without notice.

    No compensation will be provided for expired points.

  6. 6-Returns, Cancellations & Adjustments

    Returned or cancelled invoices will result in automatic deduction of related points.

    If bonus eligibility is affected by returns, Royal Media reserves the right to cancel or reverse the bonus.

  7. 7-Non-Transferability

    Points and rewards are strictly personal to the registered account.

    Points may not be transferred, sold, assigned, or combined with other accounts.

  8. 8-Fraud, Abuse & Misconduct

    Royal Media reserves the right to immediately:

    • Cancel accumulated points
    • Revoke bonus eligibility
    • Suspend or permanently terminate membership

    In cases of suspected fraud, manipulation, misuse, or violation of company policies.

  9. 9-Program Modification & Termination

    Royal Media reserves the full right to:

    • Amend reward structures
    • Modify bonus thresholds
    • Change validity periods
    • Suspend or terminate the program entirely

    At any time without prior notice.

  10. 10-Limitation of Liability

    Royal Media shall not be liable for:

    • Technical errors in point calculation
    • System downtime
    • Loss of points due to force majeure events
    • Any indirect or consequential damages arising from program participation

  11. 11-Governing Law

    These Terms & Conditions shall be governed and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the Arab Republic of Egypt.
